About Me

I am Peter Ifeanyi, a dedicated designer with over eight years of experience designing both residential and commercial spaces. I excel at crafting designs that not only show a deep understanding of client's requirements but also embrace functionality, aesthetics and sustainability. While my primary passion is design, I also enjoy the post design processes of material sample selection, vendor selection, project supervision and design reviews based on site constraints. The final handover of the project to a very satisfied client often gives that indescribable feeling of fulfillment and gives me the energy I need to navigate the not-so-rosy aspects of the industry.

Also, the collaborative nature of the design process has developed my interpersonal skills, making me able to think on my feet and effectively lead a project to achieve project objectives. My versatility is reflected in my ability to thrive in a team set up and also to excel working independently. My ability to adapt and learn quickly makes me a valuable asset in any project, ensuring that client expectations are not only met but exceeded. What I admire most about myself is my unwavering determination to get things done. Additionally, I am a budding UI/UX designer, applying my architectural background to design functional and beautiful user interfaces and experiences.
